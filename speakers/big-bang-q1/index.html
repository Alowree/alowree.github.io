FINEQ True Wireless Stereo Bluetooth Speaker Big Bang Q1

Big Bang More Possibilities

Features

Universal Bluetooth Wireless
Big Bang works with devices with Bluetooth - the wireless standard for smart devices. Stream audio directly to Big Bang without missing a beat; letting you keep your smart phone and tablet exactly where you want it - in your hand.

Exceptional Sound Performance
Designed for music lovers, Big Bang is acoustically balanced to compliment every genre, at any volume. Big Bang is crafted for natural bass response and vocal clarity; the way music is intended to be heard. The high-quality device offers 360-degree coverage and provides a deep clear sound that keeps you involved in the music for hours.

More Than Music
Not only does Big Bang bring music to life, it plays any audio from your smart phone, tablet or PC. FINEQ delivers a rich, clear audio experience for movies, TV shows, videos, games, radio and apps.

Total Wireless with Rechargeable Battery
A 7.4V 4400mAh lithium-ion battery is built-in to make Big Bang totally wireless to conveniently take anywhere around your home and in your trunk. Use any standard USB charger and type-c charging cable to juice it up.

Water-resistant IPX5

Robust, durable, and waterproof. A fixed top handle to carry it conveniently. If you are in the mood to take it on the beach, decide with confidence that the water splashes or even minor rain shower will not damage your Big Bang.

True Wireless Stereo
TWS (True Wireless Stereo) allows two Big Bang systems to be paired together to work together as one speaker system with left and right audio split between each system to fill a room with powerful, balanced sound with true stereo separation and spatial enhancement.

High Power Party Monster

RMS 74 watts high power output turns Big Bang into a real party monster. Be prepared to be frowned upon by your next door neighbours.

Audio Sources

  • Bluetooth wireless
  • FM Stereo Radio with built-in antenna
  • Aux in 3.5mm line in jack accepts input from any device with standard headphone jack
  • USB Type A receptacle for playback of music files stored in any flash driver
  • 6.5mm MIC input jack
