Personal Wearable Air Purifier RH48

Do you want to have personal protection from polluted air? If yes, you probably would consider a personal and wearable air purifier. These personal air purifiers are very quality and durable, you can use it in the car, airplane, bus, office or other small spaces.

Personal Air Purifier

The battery-powered air purifier RH48 is small-sized, portable, wearable and it does not produce noise. Very useful to clean the air around you, wherever you go. You can bring it to your office, put it in your car, wear around your neck, carry with you on the travel and more.

Ionic Technology

The personal air purifier RH48 uses ionic technology to clean the air. That technology is safe for use, it doesn’t produce a significant amount of ozone or other harmful particles. So the air purifier uses voltage to charge the molecules in the air. It releases negative ions in the air and these ions attach to the harmful particles and stop them from flying in the air.

Purification Radius

These small, battery-powered air purifiers have a small radius of cleaning. But it will release the clean air around you. Usually, they can cover at least 3 feet of space around you. That is enough if you work in a small office, or if you use it in your car.

Rechargeable Battery

The personal air purifier RH48 is small, compact, and light weight. You can wear it around your neck and have personal protection wherever you go. It is powered by a standard USB Type-C cable or by the built-in 3.7V 1000mAh lithium polymer battery, which last for 28 hours.

Features

  • 360°Purification Performance and Low Noise Level ≤30dB
  • Compact size to fit in any small areas or rooms, yet Powerful enough to deliver cleaned air 10-30m³/h
  • One button simple operations. Long press to Turn On / Off the purifier, and while the purifier is ON, short press to toggle the fan through Level 1 / Level 2 / Off.
  • Powered by USB Type C Port and Built-in Lithium-Polymer Rechargeable Battery
  • Miniature size for on-the-go handbag, backpack, or travel luggage
  • 3.7V 1000mAh big battery for 28 hours of operations
  • Versatile and Multiple use scenarios, such as in car, closet, or even your pet house
  • Add essential oil for aroma diffuser
  • Replaceable filters
  • Four color options - cool gray, red, champagne, and snow white.
