Engineering Design Services

FINEQ will provide the Engineering Design Services and Work Product described below.

FINEQ Responsibilities

  1. Meet the agreed to schedule herein specified.
  2. Advise on all electrical and mechanical BOM components and follow Customer’s direction on vendor selection.
  3. Provide Mechanical Engineering services.
  4. Provide Mechanical design, drawings, and system level integration including driving all design for manufacturing (“DFM”) process and managing related vendors during development.
  5. Complete all required product qualification including certifications and regulatory testing pursuant to Customer Qualification Plan and will provide all related test/lab documentation to Customer.
  6. Provide all required samples and prototypes pursuant to Customer qualification plan and marketing requirements.
  7. Support Customer’s designated vendor(s) and Customer engineering teams to finalize PCB dimension and components placement.
  8. Participate and Report progress on a weekly base in conference call. If primary attendee cannot attend an alternate will attend.
  9. Obtain all Regulatory Certifications specified in the Customer MRD, PRD, and or Specifications.
  10. Support the analysis of RMAs and submit RCA reports with recommendations.
  11. Adhere to the Product Change Notification process after receiving notification from Customer of such.
  12. Work with Customer team and report on EVT, DVT, PVT, and MP readiness.
  13. Close all Action items in a timely manner.
  14. Manage all supplier relations in the best interest of Customer in order to meet launch requirements.
  15. Support acquisition of MDDS reports.

Customer Responsibilities

  1. Provide ID design & CMF requirements.
  2. Perform full product User Acceptance Tests.
  3. Provide the Qualification Plan.

Deliverables at each stage of the Development

The following Deliverables listed above and shown below will be provided by FINEQ to Customer:

Stage Deliverables
Pre-design Provide design tradeoffs or recommendations.
Preliminary design review with Customer
Prototypes needed to support design recommendations
Quick Turn on PCBs
Design/Dev Product EE and BOM suggestions & support for component replacements. PCBA layout (Schematics and Gerber files) & PCB optimization suggestions per the ID and DFM
Mechanical Engineering Design, Drawings, CAD files
Perform product testing to validate compatibility
Product DFM
Tooling & EE parts Vendor Selection support
SMT verification w/ PCB layout
Samples as requested for EVT, DVT, PVT Builds
Test Fixtures Installation, Test, and Approval
Provide Product Change Notifications to Customer for approval
Design & testing review meetings and documentation
Functional test fixtures design, approval, and updating (HW+SW)
RMA testing and RCA to identify engineering associated issues
Qualification Complete all required product qualification testing and reports (EVT, DVT, PVT)

Pricing and Payment Terms

FINQ will provide the Services and Deliverables in this SOW for a fixed fee of $150,000. Additional costs for tooling and manufacturing, if any, will be included in the award letter between FINEQ and Customer for manufacturing. The following is a breakdown of the costs for the qualification, design and engineering Deliverables and associated project management:

Service Description Amount
ID and ME pre-design service: Provide design trade-offs or recommendations and preliminary design review with Customer to include a range of ID concepts, CMF options, and preliminary cost estimate including piece part price and MVA impact $20,000
ME Design Service: Mechanical design and engineering services including ME drawings and specifications, tooling fabrication and follow up, FAI, tooling revision and CMF refinement $30,000
EE design service: BOM suggestion & support for component replacements, PCB size optimization per the ID and manufacturability requirements $30,000
FW design service -Done by 3rd party design house $0
Qualification Service for EVT/DVT/PVT samples Not including UV Test and Shock Test, which need to be quoted separately $30,000
Overall Program Management $30,000
Line setup cost @$5,000 per production line, run-rate 50K/month @2 lines $10,000
TOTAL $150,000

FINEQ will submit invoices upon Customer’s acceptance of the invoiced Deliverable. Payment term for invoice of Services is 1st 50% upon project award and the rest 50% upon Customer’s approval to MP start.
