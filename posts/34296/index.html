User Manual Big Boom Q2

Getting Started

Package Contents

Examine whether the following package contents are all included:

  • Portable Bluetooth Speaker, 1 unit
  • Type-C charging cable, 1 piece
  • 3.5mm to 3.5mm audio cable, 1 piece
  • User Manual, 1 piece

Connecting the USB Charger

WARNING! For optimal performance, use only the supplied charging cable. USB charger is not included in the package, please charge Alcor via a standard 5V 1A or 5V 2A USB charger, or via your MacBook/PC that comes with a USB charging port.

Built-in Battery

Alcor has a built-in rechargeable battery to power without using the USB charger.

Battery status indicators – not charging:

When the System is in Power Off mode and in battery mode, a short press on POWER button will initiate a quick flash of the Battery Status Indicators, all 4 segment LEDs will be turned on and then quickly off.

Battery status indicators - during charge:

When power button is pressed while the battery is charging the 4 LEDs shall illuminate as follows:

  • 0-25% charge is 1 LED flashing
  • 25-50% is 1 LED on, 1 LED flashing
  • 50-75% is 2 LEDs on, 1 LED flashing
  • 75-99% is 3 LEDs on, 1 LED flashing
  • 100% is 4 LEDs on

Notes:

  • It takes about 5 hours to charge the battery from empty to full.
  • Do not dispose of the battery in fire; it may explode or leak.

Type: Li-Polymer Rechargeable Battery
Rated Voltage: 3.7 V
Capacity: 3000 mAh

WARNING！There is a danger of explosion if the battery is incorrectly replaced. Replace it only with the same or equivalent type. Do not expose it to excessive heat such as sunshine, fire, etc.

IMPORTANT:

When playing music during charging, please put the volume to middle, because for some music with heavy bass, the charger may not be able to afford the high current temporarily under the maximum volume.

System Overview


  1. POWER button

  2. TWS button

  3. MODE button
    When neither AUX IN jack nor USB Type-A receptacle is plugged in, press MODE button on the System repeatedly to toggle between Bluetooth mode and FM tuner mode.

  4. PLAY/PAUSE button
    In Bluetooth / AUX IN / USB Flash Drive mode, press to play or pause a track.
  5. RGB LIGHT button

  6. VOLUME UP / PREVIOUS button
    In any of the four modes, Short Press to increase the volume level.
    In Bluetooth mode, Long Press to skip a previous track.
    In FM tuner mode, Long Press to select a preset station or a frequency you desired.
    In USB mode, Long Press to skip a previous track.
    In AUX mode, Long Press will yield nothing.

  7. VOLUME DOWN / NEXT button
    In any of the four modes, Short Press to decrease the volume level.
    In Bluetooth mode, Long Press to skip a next track.
    In FM tuner mode, Long Press to select a preset station or a frequency you desired.
    In USB mode, Long Press to skip a next track.
    In AUX mode, Long Press will yield nothing.
  8. Battery Life Indicators
    4 segment LEDs
  9. USB Type-A receptacle
    Connects and plays music stored in your USB flash drive.
  10. USB Type-C charging port
  11. AUX IN jack
  12. Reset button

Selecting the Sound Source

This system enables you to access four sound sources - Bluetooth, FM tuner, AUX IN, and USB Flash Drive.

First-time Bluetooth Pairing

  • When neither AUX IN nor USB Type-A is plugged in, simply press MODE button on the System repeatedly to toggle between Bluetooth and FM tuner.
  • Under either Bluetooth or FM tuner mode, to plug in a 3.5mm-to-3.5mm audio cable into the AUX IN jack will automatically switch the sound source to AUX IN mode.
  • Similarly, under either Bluetooth or FM tuner mode, to plug in a USB Flash Drive into the USB Type-A receptacle will automatically switch the sound source to USB mode.
  • In terms of priority
    • Bluetooth, FM tuner - Low
    • AUX IN, USB - High

Auto Bluetooth Pairing

When the System is powered on, with neither AUX IN nor USB Type-A being plugged in, they System will automatically connect the last paired Bluetooth device. Press PLAY button either on the System or on the App of the Bluetooth device to play audio content.

When the System is powered on, with either AUX IN nor USB Type-A being plugged in, they System will automatically connect the last paired Bluetooth device. Press PLAY button either on the System or on the App of the Bluetooth device to play audio content. Under this setup with three sources available, press MODE button to toggle among FM tuner, USB or AUX IN, and Bluetooth.

When the System is powered on, with both AUX IN and USB Type-A being plugged in, they System will automatically connect the last paired Bluetooth device. Press PLAY button either on the System or on the App of the Bluetooth device to play audio content. Under this setup with all four sources available, press MODE button to toggle among FM tuner, USB, AUX IN, and Bluetooth.

Basic Operations

Turning on/off the System

While the System is in OFF mode, long press POWER button to turn on the System.

  • The blue LED above POWER button will flash slowly, 1s on and 1s off, indicating System is ready for Bluetooth pairing.
  • Battery status indicators
    • while not charging, will show current remaining battery charge.
      • 0-3% charge is 1 LED flashing
      • 3-25% charge is 1 LED on
      • 25-50% is 2 LEDs on
      • 50-75% is 3 LEDs on
      • 75-100% is 4 LEDs on
    • while during charge, will light on by 1 LED on, 2 LEDs on, 3 LEDs on, 4 LEDs on, and 4 LEDs off cyclically to indicate charging in progress.
  • RGB Light will glow slowly.

While in ON mode, long press POWER button to turn off the System.

Adjusting the Volume

When one of sound sources is selected, you can adjust the volume level from 0 to 16 by short pressing the (VOLUME UP) and (VOLUME DOWN) buttons.

Bluetooth

Establishing a Bluetooth Connection

Notes:

  • If your mobile phone supports A2DP protocol, its music will play through the system.
  • You can make a Bluetooth connection with a mobile phone, a tablet, a personal computer, or any other Bluetooth enabled devices.
  • Before the pairing process, please keep both AUX IN jack and USB Type-A receptacle unplugged
  1. Long press POWER button to turn on the System. The blue LED above POWER button will flash slowly, 1s on and 1s off, indicating System is ready for Bluetooth pairing.

  2. Tap MODE button repeatedly to toggle between Bluetooth and FM tuner, and make sure to select the Bluetooth mode. If this is the first time set up, in Bluetooth mode, the System will remain silent, while in FM tuner mode, the System will produce some static noise since no preset radio station has been saved yet.

  3. During this period, you can operate the mobile phone or Bluetooth enabled device to pair with the system within a 33 feet (10m) range.

  4. Once the System is detected and its name GW521 appears on the display of your Bluetooth enabled device, select and pair.
  5. Once the Bluetooth connection is established successfully, the blue LED indicator will be solid on.
  6. Select the desired music and press PLAY button on your device to playback the music.

Memorized Bluetooth Device

Alcor speaker can memorize the last paired Bluetooth device. When Alcor speaker is powered on (and switched to Bluetooth mode by default), and if the last paired device is in range, a Bluetooth connection between the two will be automatically restored.

Breaking a Bluetooth Connection

Suppose you have your Alcor speaker paired with one of your mobile phones, but now you’d like to pair it with another mobile phone instead. At a time, only one Bluetooth connection can be maintained, therefore in order for you to pair Alcor speaker with another mobile phone, you will have to disconnect it with the existing paired one in the first place. To break an existing Bluetooth connection, please press TWS button on the top of Alcor speaker. Once the connection is broken, the blue above POWER button will start blinking. Now you could pair it with another mobile phone.

FM Tuner

Listening to a Station

Note: Position the antenna as far away as possible from the AC adaptor, a TV, or other radiation source.

  1. Long press POWER button to turn on the System and enter Bluetooth mode by default.
  2. Press MODE button repeatedly to toggle between Bluetooth and FM tuner, and make sure to select the FM tuner mode before the next step. If this is the first time set up, in Bluetooth mode, the System will remain silent, while in FM tuner mode, the System will produce some static noise since no preset radio station has been saved yet.

  3. In FM tuner mode, with two possible scenarios

    1. If this is the first time of set up, long press PLAY/PAUSE button to automatically scan frequency (from 87.5 MHz to 108.0 MHz) and store all identified stations.
    2. If you already have preset stations or you’ve completed the step above, now you can select a desired radio station for listening. Simply long press or button to toggle among the previously stored stations.

  4. If necessary, adjust the volume level.
    Short press button increase the volume level.
    Short press button to decrease the volume level.
    Short press PLAY/PAUSE button to mute the speaker.

External Device

AUX IN

You can enjoy the sound from an optional component such as a digital music player through the speakers of this unit. Be sure to turn off the power of each component before making any connections. For details, refer to the instruction manual of the component to be connected.
Connect an external device to the AUX IN jack on the back panel of the System using a 3.5mm-to-3.5mm stereo audio cable.

  1. Long press POWER button to turn on the System.
  2. Under either Bluetooth or FM tuner mode, to plug in a 3.5mm-to-3.5mm audio cable into the AUX IN jack will automatically switch the sound source to AUX IN mode, and the System will play music files stored in the USB drive automatically. RGB Light will also be turned on automatically.

USB Drive

  1. Long press POWER button to turn on the System.

  2. Under either Bluetooth or FM tuner mode, to plug in a USB Flash Drive into the USB Type-A receptacle will automatically switch the sound source to USB mode, and the System will play music files stored in the USB drive automatically. RGB Light will also be turned on automatically.

  3. Long press or button to select songs you like, short press to adjust the volume up and down.

  4. Press PLAY/PAUSE button to play or pause.

TWS - True Wireless Stereo

  1. Turn on two identical speakers you want to pair, both under BT mode. Only need to set one of the two units.

  2. Short-press the “TWS” button to pair, the TWS button will keep lighting after paired successfully.

  3. Make Bluetooth connection with your mobile phone to play music by the two paired Speakers to create more powerful surround sound effect.

  4. Short-press the “TWS” button again to exit TWS mode.

RGB Light

The RGB light is always auto on whenever Alcor speaker is powered on. User can short press the RGB Light button to toggle among the Lighting Mode 1, Lighting Mode 2, Lighting Mode 3, and OFF.

Troubleshooting

Reset to Factory Default

If the speaker does not turn off or respond, user can short-press the Reset jack with a small round pin to reset the speaker.
