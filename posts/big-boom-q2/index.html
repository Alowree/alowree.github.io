FINEQ announces the launch of Big Boom Q2

Created2021-10-19|Updated2021-11-03
|Word count:813|Reading time:5min|Post View:

FINEQ DELIVERS EXCEPTIONAL SOUND, IN A COMPACT, PORTABLE DESIGN FOR THE OUTDOOR WITH BIG BOOM

FINEQ, maker of the critically acclaimed line of wireless speakers, today announces the launch of Big Boom, a portable, wireless outdoor speaker that delivers impressive sound in a moderate size with the convenience of Bluetooth wireless. With a on-the-go design inspiration, Big Boom offers a clean, modern look, unique to other portable speakers, and complements home décor. Starting today, Big Boom will be available in two versions: an all black and an all gray at a competitive retail price of $69. FINEQ will be launching additional colors at the end of Q4 2021.

Design Notes

In our experience many people enjoy bringing a portable speaker with them when engaging outdoor activities, so we wanted to design a portable speaker specifically for the outdoor enthusiasts. From the acoustics to the design details, Big Boom is a thoughtfully crafted way to stream music from your phone to enjoy, share and ‘decorate’ your journey with music. It’s a great companion speaker while you are walking, hiking, working out, tailgating, relaxing on the patio or entertaining guests in your backyard.

With a pair of custom-made full-range drivers, a tweeter, and a pair of carefully tuned passive raditors, Big Boom is acoustically balanced to complement anyone’s musical taste. Big Boom is ready to bring anywhere on-the-go with its easy-to-carry handle and built-in rechargeable Lithium-ion battery for up to seven hours of AC-free listening. It also includes a Type-C USB charging port so users can charge their Big Boom with any ubiquitous Type-C cables, at home, in office, or in any hotel room.

With today’s unlimited access to streaming music and video services, Big Boom allows users to bring their music, TV shows and movies to life and share their listening experience with friends and family. Big Boom continues to serve FINEQ’s commitment to delivering a versatile, wireless audio entertainment experience, and is packed with features including:

  • 2.1Ch acoustically balanced sound
  • Built-in rechargeable battery offers up to five hours of AC-free listening
  • Robust carrying handle, a convenient design for on-the-go use applications
  • Universal Type-C USB charging port to charge with any ubiquitous Type-C cables anywhere
  • Line-in available to plug and play any device with a standard headphone jack
  • Music playback for audio files stored in a USB flash drive

  • FM radio

  • Water resistance level IPX5, splash-proof and rainproof, perfectly designed for outdoor scenarios such as mounted on a bike, in a boat, on the beach, at the poolside, and more

  • TWS - True Wireless Stereo, allows for two Big Boom systems to be paired together to play as one speaker system with left and right audio split between each for true stereo separation and powerful sound.

  • Three different modes of RGB Lighting
