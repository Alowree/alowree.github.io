Features

  • PORTABLE MINI HUMIDIFIER: The contemporary design with small footprint takes up minimal space leaving ample room on your nightstand or office desk. Ideal for travel too.
  • DUAL MIST MODES: Short press the button once to activate Continuous Spray for about 5 hours working time per full tank. Short press the button again to enter Intermittent Spray (spay 3s and stop 3s) . Short press the button a third time to Power Off the humidifier.
  • ONE BUTTON CONTROL: The portable USB powered humidifier is super easy to operate. One button controls for all functions, you simply need to press one button to set the mist. Make your life easier.
  • AROMA HUMIDIFIER / DIFFUSER WITH ESSENTIAL OILS: Great for removing odors and contaminants, getting rid of cooking smells in your living room. Customize with your own essential oils to fill your home with fragrance - Peppermint, Lavender, Lemongrass, Sweet Orange, Tea Tree, or Eucalyptus.
  • DOUBLE WATER FILTRATION: Filters the water supply prior to evaporation. Preferably designed to remove impurities such as minerals, lead, chlorine and organic compounds from the water so that these contaminants are not expelled into the environment or negatively affect the humidifier. ①Two micro containers for ceramic balls ②Two cotton swabs
  • POWER VIA USB-C CABLE: You could use either the included cable or just any ubiquitous USB type-C cable
  • BUYER’S TIPS: ①Do not use tap water or distilled water, which will cause the cotton swab to clog and then humidifier unusable. ②Soak the cotton swab for a few minutes before you turn the humidifier on. ③Any further questions, please do feel free to contact us.

Getting to know your Humidifier

User Guide

Install or replace the Cotton Swab

Remove Cotton Swab: Press the two buckles on the lower sides of the humidifier to open the main housing, unplug the swab tubes and remove the cotton swabs.

DO NOT accidently take the springs out of the swab tubes, or the humidifier may not work properly.

Install Cotton Swab: Soak the cotton swabs for 5-10 minutes before installing them into the swab tubes.

The impurities of water will clog the cotton swab after a long term of use. We recommend you to replace the filter cotton swabs every 1-3 months or replace them when you find the humidifier mist amount to be too little.

Install or replace the Ceramic Balls

  1. Twist off the swab tube and ceramic ball container as illustrated

  2. Put ceramic balls into the container

  3. Screw tight the swab tube and ceramic ball container

DO NOT install the ceramic balls accidently into the spring slot, or the humidifier may not work properly.

Refill with Essential Oil or replace the Aroma Ball

  1. Rotate the cover counter-clockwise to open it
  2. Drip the ball with your favorite essential oil or perfume and then put the ball back into the slot
  3. Screw the cover back on

Customize with your own essential oils to fill your home with fragrance - Peppermint, Lavender, Lemongrass, Sweet Orange, Tea Tree, or Eucalyptus - Absolutely to your personal preference. Please remember to replace with a new ball when you would like a change to different types of aroma.

Refill the Water Tank

Press the two buckles on the lower sides of the humidifier to remove the main housing, then refill water to the MAX level (600mL). Put the housing back on and squeeze a little bit, and the buckles will snap fit with the water tank. Please operate with care and DO NOT spill water into the charge port.

DO NOT use tap water or mineral water. Tap water has many minerals swimming in it. Among these minerals are lead, calcium, and magnesium. After they deposit in the bottom, humidifiers expel them into the air in the form of white dust. The dust eventually rests on your furniture and surfaces and you breathe it in without noticing. The whole process can compromise your health by causing harm to your lungs. Tap water promotes bacterial growth in your humidifier. It then allows your humidifier to spray microbes and minerals into the air that you breathe. This can be harmful to children with breathing issues such as asthma.

Distilled or demineralized water is the best for humidifiers. Due to the distillation process it goes through, it has very low mineral content. As a result, it allows fewer microbes to form in your humidifier. These microbes include bacteria, viruses, and mold.

Toggle the Mist Mode

After the power cord is being plugged in, the blue LED indicator will turned on. ①Short press the button once to activate Continuous Spray mode for about 5 hours working time per tank. ②Short press the button the second time to enter Intermittent Spray mode (spay 3s and stop 3s) , which would provide about 10 hours working time. ③Short press the button a third time to Power Off the humidifier. Simply put, short press the button to toggle through Continuous Spay, Intermittent Spray, and Power Off.

Turn On / Off the Night Light

Whenever the power cord is being connected and plugged in, long press the button for 3 seconds, regardless of the working modes,Continuous Spay, Intermittent Spray, or Power Off, would Turn On the night light. Long press again, to Turn Off the night light.

Use Environment

  • Install the system in a location which is solid and level, and the humidifier shall be placed horizontally and stable.
  • Do not install near any heat sources such as radiators, heat registers, stoves, or other apparatus (including amplifiers) that produce heat. Avoid direct sunlight.
  • Install the system in a location which is away from furniture, wall, or other electric apparatus, as they might be spoiled by dampness.
